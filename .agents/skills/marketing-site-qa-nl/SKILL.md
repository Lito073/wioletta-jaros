# Marketing Site QA NL

Use this skill for UI, content, and QA work on the public website.

## Check against the PRD

- Compare changed content and layout with `docs/PRD.md`.
- Keep all customer-facing text in Dutch.
- Keep the primary CTA focused on quote requests and `/contact`.
- Preserve the business positioning: zakelijke schoonmaak in regio Altena.

## QA expectations

- Verify touched pages on desktop and mobile.
- Use Playwright for navigation, CTA, and accessibility checks when UI changes.
- Watch for accessibility regressions, missing headings, broken links, and inconsistent company details.
- Keep visual choices intentional and avoid generic defaults.
